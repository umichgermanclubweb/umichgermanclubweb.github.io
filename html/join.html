Join German Club!

German Club is open to any University of Michigan student looking to improve their German language skills and/or interested in learning more about German-speaking culture. We have everyone from 101 students to advanced students and native speakers participating in club events, and there is something that everyone contributes to the club. There are no membership dues for German Club, and you are welcome to come to as many events as you like: check out our next event or join our mailing list for more information.

Check us out on Facebook, Instagram, and Maize Pages!
